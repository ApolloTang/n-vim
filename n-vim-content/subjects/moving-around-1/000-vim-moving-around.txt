0       to the beginning of the current line
^       to the 1st non-whitespace character on the current line
$       To the end of the current line
w       To the beginning of next word || to next punctuation characters
W       To the beginning of next word (ignore puctuation)
b       To the beginning of previous word || to previous punctuation characters.
B       To the beginning of previous word, ignoreing punctuation characters



:: goto

{num}G   goto line
G       goto last line
1G      goto frist line
